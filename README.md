# 🍫 CECAB Transparency Platform
**A high-performance portal for the CECAB Cooperative, focused on Fair Trade, Organic Cacao sustainability, and supply chain transparency.**

[cite_start][cite: 19] [cite_start]Built with **Next.js**, **TypeScript**, and **Tailwind CSS**, this platform serves as the digital window for international stakeholders and auditors to verify the organic cacao supply chain. [cite: 19, 20] [cite_start]The project leverages **Server-Side Rendering (SSR)** to ensure that sustainability reports and cooperative news are indexed correctly by search engines and load instantly worldwide. 

## 🚀 Engineering Highlights & Business Value

* **Sustainability Showcasing:** Developed a visually rich frontend that highlights the cooperative's growth and organic impact (2022-2024), aligned with Fair Trade standards.
* [cite_start]**Performance via SSR:**  Implemented Next.js Server-Side Rendering to handle dynamic transparency data, ensuring rapid data loading for international auditors even on limited connections.
* **Type-Safe Architecture:** Utilized **TypeScript** to define strict interfaces for supply chain data, ensuring a reliable bridge between the Django multi-tenant kernel and the frontend.
* **Dynamic Content Management:** Designed to showcase real-time news, cooperative assembly updates, and partner certifications (Fair Trade, Organic) fetched from a centralized API.
* **Responsive & Accessible Design:** Built with **Tailwind CSS** to be fully responsive, ensuring cooperative members and global partners can access data on any device.

## ⚙️ Architecture Overview

The CECAB platform is a dedicated tenant of the **Centralized Government & NGO Kernel**. [cite_start]It utilizes a specialized middleware to fetch tenant-specific content while benefiting from the core's **ACID-compliant** financial logic and **PostgreSQL optimization** for transparency records.
